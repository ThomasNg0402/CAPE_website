We use a lot of sources

Usage: Wikipedia is utilized as a reference for gathering general information about COVID-19, including its history, symptoms, and global impact. Purpose: Wikipedia serves as a comprehensive knowledge base, providing accurate and detailed information to enhance the educational content on your website. It is a reliable source for understanding the background and context of the pandemic.

Usage: The Ninja API for COVID-19 is employed to fetch real-time statistics and data related to the pandemic, including global and local COVID-19 cases, deaths, and recovered cases. Purpose: Integrating the Ninja API enhances the website's functionality by providing users with up-to-date and accurate information on COVID-19 statistics. This dynamic data allows users to stay informed about the current state of the pandemic..

Usage: The heatmap from Accgis is employed as a visualization tool to represent the concentration of COVID-19 cases globally. It visually displays the intensity of cases across different regions. Purpose: The heatmap adds a visual and interactive element to the website, allowing users to explore and understand the geographical spread of COVID-19. It provides an engaging way for users to grasp the global impact of the pandemic.

Usage: Lottie animations are used to enhance the visual appeal of the website. These animations are strategically placed to communicate information, such as introducing sections, providing attention-grabbing elements, and making the website more engaging. Purpose: Lottie animations bring a dynamic and modern feel to the website, making it more interactive and user-friendly. They serve to capture the user's attention, convey information in a visually appealing manner, and contribute to an overall positive user experience.
